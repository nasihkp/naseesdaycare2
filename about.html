Our Story

A Vision for Early Education

Nasees Love & Learn Daycare & Preschool was founded with a simple yet profound vision: to provide a nurturing, safe, and stimulating environment where children can thrive during their most crucial developmental years.

Located in the heart of Mangalshery, Vellamunda, we offer a Montessori-inspired approach that balances structured learning with creative play. Our goal is to partner with parents to lay a strong foundation for lifelong learning, social-emotional well-being, and academic success.

Our Core Values

Love & Care

We treat every child with the same love, respect, and attention they receive at home.

Holistic Growth

Focusing on cognitive, physical, social, and emotional development through balanced activities.

Safety First

A secure, hygienic, and child-friendly environment equipped with modern safety measures.
